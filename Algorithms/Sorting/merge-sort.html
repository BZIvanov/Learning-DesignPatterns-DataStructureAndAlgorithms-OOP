About

The logic of the algorithm is that we split the array and then we split the already splitted arrays until we have many arrays with only 1 element. And then we sort the single elements arrays and merging them back to 1 array.

It is called Merge sort, because we merge all the single elements arrays into one sorted array.

Visualization of the algorithm

2 8 7 4 3 1 5 initial array
2 8 7 4 3 1 5 we split the initial array in two arrays
2 8 7 4 3 1 5 we keep splitting the splitted arrays until we have only arrays with 1 element
8 7 4 3 1 5 now we have all numbers as arrays with 1 element. Here we don't have the number 2 because the recursion is returning for arrays with 1 element and we don't have recursion call here
2 7 8 3 4 1 5 now we start merging to the top of the recursion, that is why we first merge 7 and 8 before merge with 2
2 7 8 1 3 4 5 here we merge again the previous arrays from the recursion
1 2 3 4 5 7 8 and now we have the final result
