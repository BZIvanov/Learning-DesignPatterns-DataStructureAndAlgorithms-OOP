Design patterns types

  • Creattional - they deal with the creation of objects. Can be explicit (constructor) and implicit (dipenedency injection, reflection, etc..). Can be also wholesale (single statement) or piecewise (step by step).
  • Structural - concerned with the structure (e.g. class members).
  • Behavioral - they are all different doing different thing, no central theme.
